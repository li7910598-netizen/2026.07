哈尔滨图论与组合研讨会

会议通知

Harbin Seminar on Graph Theory and Combinatorics

Conference Notice

尊敬的各位专家同仁：

您好！由中国运筹学会图论组合分会和哈尔滨工程大学联合主办的“哈尔滨图论与组合研讨会”将于2026年7月3—5日在黑龙江省哈尔滨市举行。我们诚挚邀请您参加本次研讨会。

本次研讨会旨在交流图论、组合及其相关应用领域的国际前沿热点和最新研究成果，推动各研究方向的交叉融合与发展，为同行提供一个学术交流的平台。

Dear Experts and Colleagues,

Greetings! Jointly organized by the Graph Theory and Combinatorics Branch of the Operations Research Society of China and Harbin Engineering University, the "Harbin Seminar on Graph Theory and Combinatorics" will be held from July 3 to 5, 2026, in Harbin, Heilongjiang Province. We sincerely invite you to attend this seminar.

This seminar aims to exchange international cutting-edge topics and the latest research achievements in graph theory, combinatorics, and their related application fields, promote the cross-integration and development of various research directions, and provide an academic exchange platform for peers.
